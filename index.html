Hey, I'm Eddy Gomez, an accomplished front-end developer from Jacksonville, FL. I've worked at Newfold Digital for 11 years, with over 3 years in a front-end developer role. I am passionate about finding creative solutions to complex problems while learning a thing or two along the way.

I've been working on different aspects of websites all through my tenure at Newfold Digital. I've helped manage e-commerce inventories, fully redesigned client websites, and even developed small web apps to boost the productivity of my peers. However, my most formative years have been in my current role as a front-end developer.

The responsibilities in my current role is to maintain the front-end of some of our biggest brands such as Web.com, Register.com and Network Solutions. I work in an agile environment to complete tasks that range in complexity. I help make sure that these websites are accessible, performant, secure, and search engine friendly.

That however is only scratching the surface, you see these websites are built using Adobe Experience Manager. Which means I also maintain the tools that we use to maintain the websites. Such as creating components that are used for authoring pages. Writing scripts to package form data to send to APIs. Even using Adobe Target to create A/B tests.
